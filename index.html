About the Netherlands

The Netherlands, also known as Holland, is a country located in Northwestern Europe. It is famous for its windmills, tulips, and picturesque canals. The country is known for its flat landscape, with a significant part of its territory situated below sea level.

Culture and Traditions

The Netherlands has a rich cultural heritage, with influences from various regions and historical periods. Dutch people are known for their tolerance, cycling culture, and love for cheese. Additionally, they celebrate colorful events such as King's Day and Sinterklaas.
